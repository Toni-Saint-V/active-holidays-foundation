# Product OS Radar · Sample

## CURRENT PRODUCT PICTURE

- AI-native surfaces already exist in `landing`, `intake`, `result`, `trust`.
- The strongest current value is deterministic guidance plus explainability.
- `npm run automations:check:screens` is the first deterministic signal before any product recommendation.

## PRODUCT CRITIQUE

- The product can look intelligent in isolated blocks, but the user still needs one obvious "what should I do next?" moment after each decision surface.
- The biggest risk is not lack of features; it is a scattered sense of guidance.

## TOP OPPORTUNITY

- Strengthen the result-to-next-action loop with one evidence-backed companion moment.
- Do not start a broad redesign; choose one surface where the user already has a decision and needs confidence.

## WHY NOW

- It upgrades perceived intelligence without requiring backend changes.
- It makes the product feel like a decision companion, not a static verdict viewer.

## RECOMMENDED MOVE

- Prepare one small brief for the highest-impact result or trust surface improvement.
- If the move touches UI hierarchy, mark it `requires_png_approval` before implementation.

## PATCH OR BRIEF

- Brief only unless the change is non-UI, copy-only, or already covered by an approved design.

## VERIFY

- Product impact, effort, reversibility, and evidence are explicit.
- No invented backend, metric, or legal/trust claim.
- Verification commands are listed in the brief.

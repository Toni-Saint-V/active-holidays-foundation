# Market Reality Product Innovation

## Goal

Protect the product from fake strategy, then generate genuinely strong ideas on top of real market constraints.

## Quick start

- Write all user-facing output in Russian.
- This skill must browse current sources before making market claims.
- Prefer official company/product pages, pricing pages, changelogs, help docs, or primary materials over summaries.
- Load `references/market-lens.md` before concluding.

## Workflow

1. Frame the decision.
   - Identify what exactly is being tested:
     - feature idea
     - product flow
     - monetization move
     - positioning
     - moat hypothesis
     - roadmap priority

2. Validate against live market reality.
   - Browse the current market.
   - Check:
     - direct competitors
     - adjacent products users compare against
     - current AI-native expectations
     - pricing and packaging patterns
     - behavior users are already trained by
   - Separate:
     - proven market pattern
     - plausible inference
     - speculation

3. Pressure-test the idea.
   - Ask:
     - Is this actually differentiated?
     - Is this commodity?
     - Does this solve a painful moment strongly enough?
     - Would users trust it?
     - Can the business capture value from it?
     - Is the timing right for this stage of the product?

4. Invent upward, not sideways.
   - After validation, propose “super things” that are:
     - meaningfully better
     - believable to build
     - coherent with the current product
     - hard to copy quickly when combined with your strengths
   - Prefer ideas that improve:
     - trust
     - speed to outcome
     - clarity
     - retained value
     - monetization quality

5. Prioritize.
   - Split output into:
     - should do now
     - strong next bet
     - bold idea for later

## Output

- `Что реально происходит на рынке сейчас`
- `Что в нашей идее слабое или commodity`
- `Где есть шанс на настоящий edge`
- `Сильные идеи`
- `Что делать сейчас`

## Rules

- Do not make market claims without current sources.
- Do not confuse novelty with defensibility.
- Do not propose “супервещи” that require a different company, team, or business model than the current one.
- Do not recommend premium complexity before the core value is trusted.
